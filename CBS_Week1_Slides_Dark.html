Why does this matter to you?

The world is changing fast. Here’s why you should care.

🌍

Every industry is being rewritten by technology

Finance, healthcare, logistics, energy — decisions that shape these industries are increasingly made with AI, governed by new regulation, and built on data infrastructure.

🧭

Knowing what’s real gives you an edge

There is enormous hype around AI, blockchain, and quantum. The ability to separate substance from hype — and to ask the right questions — is what makes someone genuinely useful in any room.

🔧

Execution matters more than technology choice

Most technology projects still fail — not from picking the wrong tool, but from bad data, missing governance, and poor judgement. This course teaches you to see those failures before they happen.

By the end: you’ll understand the technologies reshaping business, know why most bets on them fail, and have a framework to make better decisions than the people around you.

Your instructor

Practitioner background · academic foundation

Industry

30+ years on Wall Street and in Silicon Valley — as an operator, startup founder, and investor. I’ve built, funded, and guided these technologies across financial services and tech. Along the way, every hype cycle, market correction, and failed launch taught me something the previous one hadn’t. I’m still learning — and this course is built from those lessons.

What I learned

Technology shifts reward preparation. Understanding the fundamentals deeply enough to see what’s coming — and why — is something you build before you need it. There’s a difference between knowing something intellectually and understanding it viscerally, in a way that changes how you act. This course aims for the second kind.

Why this course is built this way

Theory gives you the tools. Cases show you what happens when people use them well — and when they don’t. This course gives you both, because the people who succeed in this space are the ones who know the theory and know what breaks when you apply it.

Quantum computing for non-physicists

How does quantum search work? Start with sound. A choir sings in perfect harmony — except one singer is slightly off. The quantum trick: cancel the harmony, and only the dissonance remains.

Interactive demo — click the buttons to walk through each step

Quantum search: the audio lab

Same principle, stripped down. Two tones — superposition, oracle phase-flip, diffuser interference. Listen to how the signal emerges from the noise.

Classical vs. quantum search

Classical search checks items one by one: O(N). Grover’s quantum search uses amplitude amplification to find the target in O(√N). Watch both run simultaneously on 100 items.

Click “Run Both” and watch the speedup in real time

Quantum weighted sum: signal vs. noise

Another way to see it. Classical subtraction eliminates colours one at a time. Quantum normalisation, oracle, and diffuser isolate the target signal in three steps.

Post-quantum cryptography

Why does this matter for finance? Modern encryption relies on the difficulty of factoring large numbers. Shor’s algorithm makes that easy for a quantum computer. This is the “harvest now, decrypt later” threat.
